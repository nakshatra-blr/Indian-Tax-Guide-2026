# Frequently Asked Questions

## Q: Do I need to file ITR if income is below 2.5 lakhs?
A: Not mandatory, but recommended for loan/visa applications.

## Q: Which regime is better - old or new?
A: If your deductions exceed Rs.3.75L, old regime saves more.

## Q: Can I switch between old and new regime?
A: Yes, salaried can switch every year. Business owners can switch once.
