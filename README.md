## 💰 Income Tax Slabs (FY 2025-26)

### New Tax Regime (Default)

| Income Range | Tax Rate |
|---|---|
| Up to Rs.4,00,000 | Nil |
| Rs.4,00,001 - Rs.8,00,000 | 5% |
| Rs.8,00,001 - Rs.12,00,000 | 10% |
| Rs.12,00,001 - Rs.16,00,000 | 15% |
| Rs.16,00,001 - Rs.20,00,000 | 20% |
| Rs.20,00,001 - Rs.24,00,000 | 25% |
| Above Rs.24,00,000 | 30% |

*Standard Deduction: Rs.75,000*

### Old Tax Regime (Optional)

| Income Range | Tax Rate |
|---|---|
| Up to Rs.2,50,000 | Nil |
| Rs.2,50,001 - Rs.5,00,000 | 5% |
| Rs.5,00,001 - Rs.10,00,000 | 20% |
| Above Rs.10,00,000 | 30% |

## ⚖️ New vs Old Regime Comparison

| Feature | New Regime | Old Regime |
|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Tax Rates | Lower | Higher |
| Section 80C | Not available | Rs.1.5 lakh deduction |
| HRA Exemption | Not available | Available |
| Standard Deduction | Rs.75,000 | Rs.50,000 |
| Home Loan Interest | Not available | Rs.2 lakh (Sec 24) |
| Best for | Low investments | Heavy investments/loans |

## 📋 Popular Tax Deductions (Old Regime)

| Section | Deduction | Limit |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| **80C** | PPF, ELSS, LIC, EPF, NSC, Tuition fees | Rs.1,50,000 |
| **80CCD(1B)** | NPS additional | Rs.50,000 |
| **80D** | Health Insurance premium | Rs.25,000 (self) + Rs.25,000 (parents) |
| **80E** | Education loan interest | Full amount (no limit) |
| **80G** | Donations to charitable institutions | 50% or 100% |
| **80TTA** | Savings bank interest | Rs.10,000 |
| **24(b)** | Home loan interest | Rs.2,00,000 |
| **HRA** | House Rent Allowance | Formula-based |

## 🧾 GST (Goods & Services Tax)

### GST Slabs

| Rate | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **0%** | Fresh food, milk, books, education, healthcare |
| **5%** | Packaged food, economy travel, small restaurants |
| **12%** | Processed food, business class tickets, medicines |
| **18%** | Most services, electronics, restaurants, IT services |
| **28%** | Luxury cars, tobacco, aerated drinks, cement |

### GST Types

| Type | When Applied |
|------|------|
| **CGST + SGST** | Intra-state (within same state) |
| **IGST** | Inter-state (between different states) |

## ✂️ TDS Rates

| Section | Payment Type | TDS Rate |
|---------|------|------|
| 194A | Interest (Banks) | 10% |
| 194B | Lottery/Game winnings | 30% |
| 194C | Contractor payments | 1% (Individual) / 2% (Others) |
| 194H | Commission/Brokerage | 5% |
| 194I | Rent | 2% (Plant) / 10% (Land/Building) |
| 194J | Professional fees | 10% |
| 192 | Salary | As per slab |

*TDS not deducted if annual payment is below threshold limit*

## 🎯 Tax Saving Tips

1. **Max out 80C** — PPF + ELSS + EPF = Rs.1.5 lakh
2. **Health insurance** — Get Rs.75,000 deduction (self + parents)
3. **NPS** — Extra Rs.50,000 under 80CCD(1B)
4. **Education loan** — Full interest deductible under 80E
5. **Compare regimes** — Use a calculator before choosing
